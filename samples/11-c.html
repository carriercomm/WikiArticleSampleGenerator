Welcome Back is a 2015 Indian action comedy film directed by Anees Bazmee, and produced by Firoz Nadiadwala.[4] It features an ensemble cast that includes Anil Kapoor, Nana Patekar, John Abraham, Shruti Hassan, Paresh Rawal, Dimple Kapadia and Naseeruddin Shah.[5][6] While Kapoor, Patekar and Rawal have reprised their roles from the 2007 film Welcome,[7] Ambraham and Shruti Haasan are new additions to the lead cast.[8] Made on a budget of US$16 million, the film was majorly shot in and around Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Welcome Back released worldwide on 4 September 2015.[9][10]
